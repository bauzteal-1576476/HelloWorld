What is TRJK?

Cali Kids with a Dream

TRJK is a company started by a group of local friends in Stockton, California. TRJK was made to provide the best skateboard equipment to people around the nation. Whether, this is shoes, wheels, backpacks, or the a dope board. TRJK has your back!
